How does it works?

Ethereum distributed wallet generate recovery keys that you can give to your friends and family, put in the cloud, etc. If you lost your Ethereum wallet, you just need have to get the keys.
Some key are missing? No problem, you don't need all the key to recover your wallet.

Is it secure and reliable?

Yes, only if you respect the the following rules:

  • Participants (Individual, systems, cold storage, etc.) will ensure confidentiality, integrity and availability of the recovery keys in "best effort".
  • Use DIFFERENT channels to communicate the recovery keys to each participant (hand-to-hand, encrypted chat/mail, etc.)
  • DELETE any copy of the recovery key once it is received by participants (Don't forget mail and chat history).
  • TEST the recovery process on a regular basis. It can also be an opportunity to regenerate the keys
  • ENSURE that every participant understand the criticality of the request.
